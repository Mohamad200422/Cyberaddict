Basic Cyber Safety Tips

  • Never share your OTP, password or UPI PIN with anyone.
  • Always check the website URL – it should start with https://.
  • Do not click on unknown links from SMS, WhatsApp or email.
  • Use strong passwords with letters, numbers and symbols.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
